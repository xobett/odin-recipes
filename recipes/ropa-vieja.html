ROPA VIEJA

Delicious stripped beef with an incredible flavor! This cuban dish's name translated to english means "Old furniture". Its meaning lies within a spanish tale, where a genie turned a poor man's old clothes into beef to feed his family.

This dish is recommended to be eaten with white rice and fried bananas!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 lb of beef
  • 1 beef flavored bouillon
  • 2 laurel leafs
  • Pinch of black pepper
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 big chopped piece of onion
  • 1/2 of a pepper
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of oregano
  • 1/2 cup of red wine
  • 1/8 cup of dry wine
  • 1/2 cup of tomato puree
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

PREPARATION

Once you have gathered all the necessary ingredients, follow the next steps to prepare your ropa vieja:

  1. Fill a pressure pot with water and add the beef. Make sure the water covers the beef for about 3 fingers above it Add salt and let it boil for about 30 minutes, or until the beef gets tendered enough to strip it
  2. Drain the beef in a container and let it chill for a bit
  3. With two forks, strip the beef into several strips
  4. To prepare the sauce, crush the garlic cloves and chop the onion and pepper into little pieces. Stir-fry them for about 30 seconds with high heat and the tablespoon of vegetable oil
  5. Add the cumin, oregano, red wine, dry wine and tomato puree to the stir-frying. Blend everything for about 30 seconds
  6. In a big stove, add the stripped beef and serve the sauce on it. Let it cook in medium heat for a few minutes
  7. Add salt to the taste
  8. Serve yourself a delicious plate of ropa vieja and enjoy!
