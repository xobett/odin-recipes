ARROZ CONGRI


Tasty cuban rice that will satisfy your hunger without a doubt! This dish is a combination of white rice, black beans, bacon and spices! "Arroz congri" translated to english means "Rice with gray" based on the gray color the rice gets at the end!

INGREDIENTS

To prepare this tasty rice, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 2 cups of white rice
  • 1 cup of black beans
  • 4 mashed garlic cloves
  • 5 pieces of pork cracklings
  • 1 medium chopped onion
  • 1/2 of chopped red/green pepper
  • 1/2 of a ground oregano tablespoon
  • 1 chopped piece of bacon
  • 1 chopped sausage
  • 3 tablespoon of vegetable oil (olive oil is good as well)
  • Salt to taste

PREPARATION

Once you have gathered all the necessary ingredients, follow the next steps to prepare your cuban rice:

  1. Wash the black beans, and place them on a pot, where you will add 3 cups of water, laurel and ground oregano, until the beans get soft. A rule thumb is to not let the beans get too soft, otherwise they could get thwarted
  2. When the beans get soft, remove the pot from the heat and lay them aside
  3. Then wash throughtly the rice and let it drain before laying it aside
  4. In a pot, stir-fry the garlic, onion, pepper, bacon, pork cracklings, sausage and some cumin for about 5 minutes
  5. After done stir-frying, add the washed rice and blend everything. After that, move everything to a rice cooker
  6. Finally, add the black beans with its broth , add salt to taste and let it cook for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Serve and enjoy!
