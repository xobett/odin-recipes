MIGHTY PROTEIN SHAKE


Delicious protein shake that will give you the necessary energy to go thorugh your morning routine! This shake contains, protein, oat, wallnuts and chia! Prepare it with your favorite protein flavor!

INGREDIENTS

To prepare this delicious shake, you will need the following ingredients:

  • 1 cup of unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 scoop of protein powder
  • 100 gm of oat
  • 12 wallnuts
  • 1 spoon of chia seeds

PREPARATION

Once you have gathered the necessary ingredients, prepare the shake following the next steps:

  1. Place the almond milk on a blender
  2. Add the protein scoop of your favorite flavor
  3. Add the oat
  4. Add the wallnuts
  5. Add a spoon of chia seeds
  6. Blend everything for about 30 seconds
  7. Serve everything on a cup and enjoy!
